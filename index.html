What is Persona 4 Revival?

Persona 4 Revival is the announced remake of Persona 4, the classic ATLUS RPG originally released in 2008. The remake was officially announced by SEGA/ATLUS in June 2025.

Release Information

The official release date is currently listed as TBD. SEGA announced that Persona 4 Revival is coming to Xbox Game Pass, Xbox Series X|S, Windows, PlayStation 5, and Steam.

Expected Improvements

  • Modern graphics and rebuilt environments
  • Updated character models
  • Smoother gameplay systems
  • A new way for fans to experience Inaba

Why Fans Are Excited

Persona 4 is known for its mystery story, small-town setting, strong characters, and emotional themes about truth and identity. Revival gives both new and returning players a chance to experience the game in a modern form.
